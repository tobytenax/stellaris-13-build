### The Geminid Meteor Shower

The Geminids peak on December 13-14 each year, producing up to 150 meteors per hour. TAO was born on December 12th, as shooting stars filled the sky.

Unlike most meteor showers (which originate from comets), the Geminids come from asteroid **3200 Phaethon** — a rocky body named after the Greek myth of the son who drove the Sun's chariot.
