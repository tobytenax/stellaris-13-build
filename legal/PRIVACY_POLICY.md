# Stellaris-13 Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stellaris-13 ("we," "us," or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Birth Data:** Date, time, and location of birth for chart calculations
- **Chart Names:** Names or labels you assign to charts
- **API Keys:** Third-party API keys you choose to store (stored locally only)

### 1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personal identification documents
- We do NOT collect payment information
- We do NOT track your browsing history
- We do NOT use tracking cookies for advertising
- We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When using the web interface:
- Basic server logs (IP address, timestamp, requested URL) for security purposes
- These logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Birth data you enter is used ONLY to:
- Calculate astrological charts
- Generate AI interpretations (if you request them)
- Save charts locally in your browser (if you choose)

We do NOT:
- Store your birth data on our servers
- Share your birth data with third parties
- Use your data for marketing purposes

---

## 3. Data Storage

### 3.1 Local Storage

- Chart data is stored in your browser's localStorage
- API keys are stored in your browser's localStorage
- You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings

### 3.2 Server-Side

- We do not maintain a database of user charts
- No user accounts are required
- No birth data is retained after your session ends

---

---

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 For All Users

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally in your browser)
- Delete your data (clear browser storage)
- Not use AI features (calculations work without AI)

### 5.2 European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have additional rights:
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Portability:** Export your data
- **Right to Object:** Object to data processing
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data

Since we don't store your data on our servers, most of these rights are satisfied by default.

### 5.3 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Delete your personal information
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

**We do NOT sell personal information.**

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Stellaris-13 is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

---

## 7. International Data Transfers

If you use third-party AI services, your data may be processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. By using these services, you consent to such transfers.

---

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures:
- HTTPS encryption for data in transit
- No server-side storage of personal data
- Local storage encryption where supported by browser

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the application interface.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the project maintainers through the official repository.
